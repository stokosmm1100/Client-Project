About

Welcome to Inside Outfit Drift Team! Born in the heart of Eau Claire in 2014, we're not just a drift collective; we're a family united by our passion for style, speed, and skillful driving. Here, we blend the art of fashion with the adrenaline rush of fast cars, creating a unique synergy that fuels our drive for excellence. With a deep-rooted love for sleek designs, roaring engines, and the thrill of precision driving, we carve our own path on the asphalt, pushing boundaries with every turn. Join us as we harness the power of good style, fast cars, and impeccable driving to leave a lasting impression on the drifting world
